Yinzheng Chen's GEOG 450 Final Portfolio

Hello! This miniature site curates the six projects I completed for GEOG 450, from hand drawn “Dear Data” trails to color analytics of UW's Quad. Use the nav bar above to explore each project.

  • Dear Data — Weekly Trail : a hand drawn data postcard tracking every trip I took in a week.
  • Welcome to Seattle : a Sankey diagram revealing the city's migration streams.
  • What's Happening in the Town : a radial map meets restaurant guide for the International District.
  • Gray Anatomy : a color driven exploration of UW's Quad after cherry blossom season.
  • Happy Places : treemap & bubble pack visualizations mapping where a novel's characters find joy.
